What does the process look like?

Process is everything in an outstanding piece and I'm proud to include two rounds of revisions based on your input. Depending on the project, the expected turn around for a piece is 2-4 weeks.

  • Understanding Confirming details and reference photos with you Initial deposit paid
  • Sketches & Development I will develop compositional sketches and confirm colors/materials with you This is the first round of revisions
  • The Making I will get to work andstart making the final piece! Upon completion, I will contact you about any minor edits or changes you'd like to make. This is the second round of revisions. With your input, I will add the final touches for....
  • Hand-Off We've crossed the finish line! You have a wonderful new piece by yours truly. I will package your piece in the formats you specified at the start. I will also be cheering and clapping my hands in celebration.
