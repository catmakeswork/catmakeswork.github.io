CAT MAKES WORK

Cat Hong (RISD) is a San Francisco based industrial designer who celebrates curiosity and learning through design. She crafts human-centric experiences that inspire and delight for all ages. She welcomes the next challenge in product design, experiences, motion design, and illustration with zest and an open sketchbook.

Her many joys include (but are not limited to) baking babka, observing squirrels, roller skating, swimming laps, animating 3D sketches, and squeezing kinetic clay.

Feel free to say: “Hi!” / “Like!” / “Let’s Work!”
