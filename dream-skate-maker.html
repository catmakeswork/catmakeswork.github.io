Welcome to Dream Skate Maker – where you can visualize and plan your ideal roller skate set-up. This was my first solo project exploring the Spline App.

Controls

Keyboard
  • Left and Right arrow keys: Spin your skate 360.
  • Numbers 1-9: Change the color of your skate boot. You can also change the color of the tongue by holding shift.
Mouse
  • Clicking pie slices on the wheel: Change the color of your wheels.
  • Clicking knots: Change the color of your laces.
