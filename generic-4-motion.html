Hallows Bugs

Client: Personal Project
My Role: Designer, Animator, Storyboard

For Halloween, I animated an eerie greeting featuring some of nature’s original spooksters: the owl moth, rhinoceros beetle, fireflies, black widow, praying mantis, and earthworm. Utilizing Instagram’s carousel format, I wanted to experiment with storytelling on a specific platform.



See if you can spot all these critters!
