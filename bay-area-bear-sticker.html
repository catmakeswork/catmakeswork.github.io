Bay Area Bear Stickers

During the pandemic I decided on two things that would change my life: moving to the Bay Area and picking up roller skating. With a start on wobbly legs supporting a fighting spirit, I couldn’t have ever imagined finding a skating community that I owe so much of my grief, growth, and gratitude. As a kind of love letter, I designed a sticker to represent wheels of the Bay – skate boarders, inliners, and quad skaters – with feedback from the community.
